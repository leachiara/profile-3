Welcome to Esslingen

Esslingen in southern Germany is an attractive town full of half-timbered buildings in the region around Stuttgart. It is also known as Esslingen am Neckar. It is a beautiful city, mostly known for it's famous Christmas market, which attracts thousands of tourists every year.
